Upcoming Festivals & Rituals

Losar Festival

The Tibetan New Year celebrated with vibrant rituals and dances in Sikkim's monasteries. Experience mask dances, music, and local cuisine.

Bumchu Festival

A sacred ritual held at Tashiding Monastery, marking prosperity and peace. The holy water pot is opened to predict the fortunes of Sikkim for the year.

Saga Dawa

Saga Dawa, celebrated with great fervor in Sikkim, is one of the most significant festivals in the region. Observed during the fourth month of the Tibetan lunar calendar, it commemorates the birth, enlightenment, and parinirvana (death) of Lord Buddha.
