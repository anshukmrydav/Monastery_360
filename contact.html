Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a guided tour of the monasteries?

You can book guided tours directly through our website by visiting the specific monastery page and using the booking form. Select your preferred date, time, and the number of visitors, then complete the payment through our secure RazorPay gateway.

Are the virtual tours free to access?

Yes, all virtual tours on our website are free to access. We believe in making cultural heritage accessible to everyone. However, if you wish to download content for offline use or book physical guided tours, there may be associated fees.

What languages are available for the audio guides?

Our audio guides are currently available in English, Hindi, Nepali, Tibetan, and Bengali. We are continuously working to add more languages to make our content accessible to a wider audience.

How can I contribute to the Monasteries 360° project?

We welcome contributions in various forms, including historical information, photographs, translations, and technical expertise. If you'd like to contribute, please contact us through the form on this page with details about how you'd like to help.

What is the best time to visit Sikkim's monasteries?

The best time to visit Sikkim's monasteries is from March to May (spring) and October to December (autumn) when the weather is pleasant and skies are clear. Many monasteries also host colorful festivals during these periods. Winter visits (December to February) can be challenging due to snow, while the monsoon season (June to September) may have limited accessibility due to rain.
